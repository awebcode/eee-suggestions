# Electronic Devices & Circuits - Exam Preparation Guide

A comprehensive exam preparation resource for **Diodes and Transistors** (BJT & MOSFET) designed for BSc CSE/EEE students at Uttara University.

## About

This project provides a well-structured, professionally designed HTML document containing 60 marks worth of questions and answers covering fundamental topics in electronic devices. Created to help students prepare effectively for their final examinations.

**Author:** Asikur Rahman  
**Institution:** Uttara University  
**Purpose:** Educational resource for fellow students

## Topics Covered

### Section A: Diodes (10 Marks)
- Definition and Symbol
- Types of Diodes (PN Junction, Zener, LED, Photodiode, etc.)
- V-I Characteristics with detailed curve analysis
- Applications in circuits

### Section B: BJT Transistors (20 Marks)
- BJT Definition with NPN and PNP symbols
- Three Configurations (Common Emitter, Common Base, Common Collector)
- Operating Modes (Active, Saturation, Cutoff, Inverse Active)
- Types and Characteristics
- Applications in amplification and switching

### Section C: MOSFET (8 Marks)
- MOSFET definition with symbols
- Detailed BJT vs MOSFET comparison table
- Key differences and applications

### Section D: Problem Solving (13 Marks)
- Current gain, amplification factor calculations
- Complete circuit analysis with step-by-step solutions
- Important formulas and typical values

### Section E: Additional Topics (9 Marks)
- Transistor as Amplifier
- Transistor as Switch
- Biasing Techniques
- Early Effect
- Advantages and Disadvantages

## Study Tips

1. **Start with Basics**: Begin with diode definitions and characteristics
2. **Understand BJT Thoroughly**: Focus on configurations and operating modes
3. **Practice Problems**: Work through the mathematical examples multiple times
4. **Use Comparison Tables**: Memorize BJT vs MOSFET differences
5. **Review Formulas**: Keep the formula section handy during practice
